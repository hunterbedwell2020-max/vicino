# Vicino Community Guidelines
Version: v1.0  
Effective date: February 18, 2026

## Core Rules
- Be respectful and truthful.
- Keep interactions consensual and age-appropriate (18+ only).
- Use public meetup locations and follow in-app safety flow.

## Not Allowed
- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or coercive behavior.
- Catfishing, impersonation, fake verification, or fraud.
- Sexual exploitation, extortion, or blackmail.
- Attempts to move first meetings to homes/private residences through Vicino prompts.
- Spam, scams, or commercial solicitation.

## Safety Expectations
- Meet in populated public places.
- Share only information you are comfortable sharing.
- Report suspicious or abusive behavior immediately.

## Enforcement
- Violations can trigger warnings, feature restrictions, temporary suspension, or permanent bans.
- Severe safety violations may result in immediate permanent removal.
