# Vicino Privacy Policy
Version: v1.0  
Effective date: February 18, 2026

## 1. Data We Collect
- Account data: email, username, password hash.
- Profile data: name fields, age, gender, preferences, bios, photos, hobbies, prompts.
- Verification data: selfie and ID document images submitted for admin review.
- Usage data: swipes, matches, messages, meetup decisions, moderation records.
- Device and app data: push token, app/session metadata.
- Location data: approximate/device location and distance preference for local discovery.

## 2. How We Use Data
- Operate core features (matching, chat, meetup coordination).
- Safety and trust workflows (verification, moderation, abuse prevention).
- Service quality, reliability, and analytics.
- Legal compliance and fraud prevention.

## 3. Sharing
- We may share data with infrastructure and tooling providers (hosting, storage, notifications, analytics).
- We do not sell personal data.
- We may disclose data when required by law or to protect users/platform integrity.

## 4. Retention
- We retain data while accounts are active and as needed for safety, legal, and operational purposes.
- Moderation and safety records may be retained longer where necessary.

## 5. Security
- We use technical and organizational safeguards, but no system is 100% secure.
- Users are responsible for safeguarding account credentials.

## 6. Your Choices
- You can update profile data in app settings.
- You can request account deletion/support review through support channels.
- Marketing consent is optional and can be changed later.

## 7. Children
- Vicino is 18+ only and not intended for minors.

## 8. Policy Updates
- We may revise this policy and update the policy version and effective date.

## 9. Contact
- Support: support@vicino.app
